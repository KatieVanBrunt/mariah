Mariah Carey, America's Favorite Diva Sweetheart

About Mariah All Hail Mariah Tumblr
Mariah Carey: queen of the butterflies, slayer of all notes, killer of epic music videos, and top Diva is perhaps one of the best performers of all time. Some may argue that songs like "Vision of Love" or "Hero" or "Fantasy" solidified Mariah's place as one of America's top perfomers, some (myself) beg to differ that music videos like "Heartbreaker" REALLY brought Mariah to a whole new level in the United States. Further, her cameo on MTV's beloved "Cribs" furthered her fame, as she showed off her luscious home and showed us her closet, how she hops into a bathtub with a towel, and when her dog and her cat don't get along.
